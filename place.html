Colorado






My trip to Colorado was full of great experiences. In Colorado Springs, I explored the Garden of the Gods, which was incredible. Denver had a lot going on with its busy streets and interesting museums, and Aurora was a quiet, laid-back town. One of the coolest moments was visting a castle in the mountains. I also got to zipline over a huge gorge, which was very exciting. At Sand Dunes National Park, I tried sandboarding for the first time and had sand in my ears for the next week. Lastly, I went white-water rafting through some wild rapids with cool mountain views.
